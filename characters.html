Characters

  • Siri Keeton: The narrator and protagonist of the book, Siri is a "synthetic" - a human with brain damage that has been replaced with artificial intelligence. He is a linguist and translator, hired to communicate with the alien they encounter on their mission.
  • The Crew of the Theseus: The crew of the starship Theseus is made up of a diverse group of individuals, each with their own unique skills and personalities. There is Jenna Dvorak, the ship's engineer; Sergei Kuznetsov, the pilot; and Hu Cheng, the security officer, among others.
  • The Aliens: The mysterious alien beings that the crew encounters on their mission are known as the "Fireflies." They are highly advanced and seem to possess powers beyond human understanding.
